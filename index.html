Welcome to my profile! This is my personal portfolio where I share my Diary and Experiences during our Educational Tour in Cebu City and Bohol City!

GitHub Vercel

Day 1: Cebu Arrival

March 15, 2024

Our exciting journey begins! Arrived in Cebu and ready for adventure. The energy of the city is palpable, and everyone is excited for the company visits and cultural experiences ahead. Settled into the hotel and prepared for a big day tomorrow.

Day 2: Bohol Adventure

March 16, 2024

Explored the beautiful island of Bohol today. The ferry ride was an experience in itself. We saw the iconic Chocolate Hills and were amazed by the unique landscape. It's incredible to see something in person that you've only seen in textbooks.

Day 3: Tarsiers and River Cruise

March 17, 2024

Visited the Tarsier sanctuary, and it was fascinating to see the tiny primates. They are so delicate. Afterwards, we had a wonderful lunch on the Loboc River floating restaurant. The food was great, and the scenery was breathtaking. A truly memorable day.

Day 4: Company Visits

March 18, 2024

Today was all about learning. We visited several IT companies like Dynata and Mata Technologies. It was inspiring to see the real-world applications of what we're studying and to hear from professionals in the field. It gave us a lot of insight into potential career paths.
